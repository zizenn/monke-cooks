## mechanics
1. cooking with cooking tools
	1. includes fry pans, pots many things
		1. the way "cooking something will work is"
			1. there will be a separate menu overlay that appears ontop of you game that basically has the cooking mechanic or process on it
	2. knifes
		1. has a bar which contains a line in it, tap when you want to cut
			1. as progression continues the line moves faster then coming to a cap
2. ingredients
	1. you have to buy ingredients in a computer menu inside the game, this is separate for each game instance
3. campaign mode
	1. upgrading the character in campaign mode
	2. upgrading your tools in campaign mode
	3. getting better restaurant and facilities
	4. customer reviews
		1. better reviews means more people which means more revenue
4. multiplayer
	1. unlocking new characters that each have their own perks
	2. make it so that you can save progress in a multiplayer lobby
		1. example im playing with my friends and we all have to hop off, i want to be able to start again from the same point
	3. when upgrading tools each player chooses 2 tool of theirs they want to upgrade, this increases efficiency and supports the idea of having roles
5. money
	1. money is gained for the amount of dishes served per round
	2. money is used to upgrade tools, equipment and player stats
6. tools (ties closely with cooking)
	1. upgrading tools makes cooking faster and easier allowing the player to do things more efficiently
	2. after levelling up the character you can unlock more tools which also unlock a variety of dishes
7. upgrading
	1. it will work like a tree, you have to go though certain paths of upgrades to upgrade certain things, like if you want to unlock deep frying then you have to go down the pot & oil tree or something similar
	2. upgrading things in general makes them better
		1. tools
			1. make them more efficient and better
		2. ingredients
			1. make them higher quality so the customer would want to pay more
		3. equipment
			1. makes the final product better and have a higher food rating
8. food rating
	1. the food rating system says how good you cooked the dish and how much it would cost proportionate to the quality of the dish
	2. the reviews are also dependent on the food rating, i the dish is a higher quality they will leave a good review and if the rating is high enough then they can even tip you
9. order list
	1. the order list is visible in some corner of the screen
	2. makes sure that you remember what orders there are
	3. after enough orders pile up some may disappear at higher levels an force the player to remeber the order to keep reviews high
10. reviews
	1. this basically controls how many people will show up a your store and how high you can price your food before the customers stop buying them
	2. the higher the reviews the more customers are willing to pay
11. the usage menu
	1. this basically shows up inside the cooking process overlay that shows you how to do it
	2. can be turned off and automatically turns off after completeting the tutorial
12. bosses
	1. these guys appear after 5 rounds
	2. they are professional eaters that are in a competition
		1. you have to cook fast to cater to how fast they are eating or else you will fail the level
	3. this serves as a marker on how good you have to be to progress to the next levels
		1. this makes sure that people dont just start losing randomly and know when its just a skill issue
